What Makes Passwords Weak?

Passwords are typically weak due to short and predictable phrases often easily linked to personal data that can be found out by anyone with the ability and interest to look into you even a little bit. Most people have their "passwords" on instagram or whatever social media you use the most. It's commonly reused across a multitude of accounts making it a one for all as it's easy to remember when you only need 1 password to access your everything.

Examples:

Weak: Mommy12334

This is a very easy and predictable, you can easily guess this without much thought, whether this is your family or your friends even that pet you posted the other day.

Medium: Mommy12345$

While this is harder to crack it isn't impossible and rather easy, I could probably crack this within maybe a day and easily. It's important to make it harder and more secure.

Hard: L0\/e9682513

This by using symbols that aren't common such as \/ and 0 makes it harder and more complex to figure out and crack. Making this more secure and keeping your information and secrets safe.
